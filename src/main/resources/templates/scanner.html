CLASSIFICATION LEGEND
Scanner categories, badges, and descriptions
1. CPR Width
Scans FNO stocks with narrow or inside CPR. Both types classified by previous day's range as % of 20-day ADR (Average Daily Range). If prev day range ≤ threshold% of ADR (default 50%) → Small (compressed, coiled spring). Else → Large (expanded, possibly exhausted). Configurable threshold in Settings.
NS Narrow + Small (coiled spring — yesterday quiet, high breakout potential)
NL Narrow + Large (exhausted — yesterday wild, lower potential)
IS Inside + Small (coiled spring — yesterday quiet, high breakout potential)
IL Inside + Large (exhausted — yesterday wild, lower potential)
2. Opening Range
Tracks the first N minutes of trading (configurable, default 15 min). After the OR window locks, LTP vs OR High/Low determines the breakout direction. DH/DL breakouts only fire after OR locks.
OR+ Bullish — LTP above OR High
OR- Bearish — LTP below OR Low
OR= Neutral — LTP inside OR range
OR? Forming — OR window still open
3. Signal Probability
CPR breakout signals classified by alignment with weekly + daily trends. Two tiers: HPT (full qty) and LPT (half qty).
HPT High Probable Trade — weekly + daily + breakout direction all aligned (full qty)
LPT Low Probable Trade — everything else: weekly neutral, weekly/daily opposed, magnets, EV reversals (half qty)
