CLASSIFICATION LEGEND
Scanner categories, badges, and descriptions
1. CPR Width
Scans FNO stocks with narrow or inside CPR. Both types split by range z-score vs the stock's own 20-day history: z = (x − μ) / σ, where x = prior day's (H − L). Small range (z < −1.5) = genuine tight bar. Large range = normal/wide bar where close landed near the midpoint.
NS Narrow + Small Range (z < −1.5)
NL Narrow + Large Range
IS Inside + Small Range (z < −1.5)
IL Inside + Large Range
2. Opening Range
Tracks the first N minutes of trading (configurable, default 30 min). After the OR window locks, LTP vs OR High/Low determines the breakout direction. DH/DL breakouts only fire after OR locks.
OR+ Bullish — LTP above OR High
OR- Bearish — LTP below OR Low
OR= Neutral — LTP inside OR range
OR? Forming — OR window still open
3. Signal Probability
CPR breakout signals classified by alignment with weekly + daily trends. Two tiers: HPT (full qty) and LPT (half qty).
HPT High Probable Trade — weekly + daily + breakout direction all aligned (full qty)
LPT Low Probable Trade — everything else: weekly neutral, weekly/daily opposed, magnets, EV reversals (half qty)
