Scanner categories, badges, and descriptions
1. CPR Width
Scans FNO stocks with narrow or inside CPR. Both types split by range z-score vs the stock's own 20-day history: z = (x − μ) / σ, where x = prior day's (H − L). Small range (z < −1.5) = genuine tight bar. Large range = normal/wide bar where close landed near the midpoint.
NS Narrow + Small Range (z < −1.5)
NL Narrow + Large Range
IS Inside + Small Range (z < −1.5)
IL Inside + Large Range
2. Signal Probability
CPR breakout signals classified by alignment with weekly trend. Higher alignment = higher probability of follow-through.
HPT High Probable Trade (breakout aligns with weekly trend)
MPT Medium Probable Trade (weekly trend neutral)
LPT Low Probable Trade (breakout against weekly trend)
